## Main Museums:

- **National Museum of Fine Arts:** Houses the famous "Spoliarium" by Juan Luna and other masterpieces of Filipino art
- **National Museum of Anthropology:** Features the San Diego shipwreck collection and cultural artifacts from various Filipino ethnic groups
- **National Museum of Natural History:** Showcases the Tree of Life installation and Philippine biodiversity

## Highlights:

- Juan Luna's "Spoliarium" - a massive painting depicting fallen gladiators
- The Tree of Life - a stunning five-story installation in the Natural History Museum
- Ancient gold artifacts and burial jars
- Dinosaur fossils and taxidermy displays

## Visitor Information:

- **Entrance:** FREE admission to all National Museums
- **Hours:** Tuesday to Sunday, 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Closed Mondays)
- **Tip:** Photography is allowed but no flash inside galleries
