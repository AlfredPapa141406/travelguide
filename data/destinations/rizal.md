## What to See and Do:

- **Rizal Monument:** Pay tribute to the national hero at this iconic monument where he was executed in 1896
- **Japanese Garden:** Experience tranquility in this beautifully landscaped garden with koi ponds and traditional architecture
- **Chinese Garden:** Explore pagodas and bridges in this serene Chinese-inspired garden
- **National Museum Complex:** Visit nearby museums showcasing Filipino art, culture, and natural history
- **Dancing Fountains:** Enjoy the spectacular light and water show in the evenings

## Best Time to Visit:

Early morning (5-7 AM) or late afternoon (4-6 PM) to avoid the midday heat. The park is especially beautiful during sunset.

## Tips:

- Bring water and wear comfortable walking shoes
- Watch out for pickpockets in crowded areas
- Perfect spot for jogging, picnics, or relaxing walks
