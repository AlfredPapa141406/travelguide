## Main Attractions:

- **Oceanarium:** Walk through a 25-meter underwater tunnel surrounded by various marine species
- **Jellies Exhibit:** Mesmerizing display of colorful jellyfish in specially designed tanks
- **Penguin Talk:** Get up close with Humboldt penguins and learn about their habitat
- **Sea Lion Show:** Entertaining performances by trained sea lions
- **All-Star Bird Show:** Watch tropical birds showcase their talents
- **Symphony Evening Show:** Multimedia fountain show with lights and music

## Interactive Experiences:

- Fish Spa - let fish gently nibble away dead skin
- Stingray interaction pool
- Behind-the-scenes tour options

## Practical Information:

- **Hours:** 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM daily
- **Duration:** Plan for 3-4 hours to see everything
- **Tickets:** Various packages available, check website for current prices
- **Best for:** Families with children, couples, marine life enthusiasts
