## Must-Visit Attractions:

- **Fort Santiago:** A citadel first built by Spanish conquistador Miguel LÃ³pez de Legazpi, featuring dungeons and beautiful gardens
- **San Agustin Church:** The oldest stone church in the Philippines, a UNESCO World Heritage Site built in 1607
- **Manila Cathedral:** A stunning Romanesque-revival style cathedral, the seat of the Archbishop of Manila
- **Casa Manila:** A museum showcasing Spanish colonial lifestyle with authentic period furniture
- **Baluarte de San Diego:** A well-preserved bastion offering great views of the surrounding area

## How to Explore:

- Walking tour (2-3 hours)
- Bamboo bike tour (eco-friendly option)
- Kalesa (horse-drawn carriage) ride for a traditional experience

## Pro Tips:

- Wear comfortable shoes - lots of cobblestone streets!
- Bring an umbrella for sun or sudden rain
- Visit on weekdays to avoid crowds
